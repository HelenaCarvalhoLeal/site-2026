About

I am a Researcher in Machine Learning with a strong focus on Representation Learning and Fairness, alongside a robust academic background bridging Physics and Computer Science. I have a proven ability to connect theoretical physics with digital innovation.

My current work involves leveraging Vision-Language Models (VLM) for forensic analysis and developing scientific software. As a published author at ACM SIGGRAPH 2025, my expertise includes analyzing skin tone biases in virtual human rendering and building pipelines to quantify fairness and assess cross-domain robustness in deep learning models.
